Untitled (may 13th 2025)

The biggest threat to the individual comes about in the form of noise, reducing their own thoughts into the solidarity of others in a combined belief of a concealed veil. In essence, the window cracks into a spiderweb of essence.


Spotify AB ©’s CEO has gone on record to state their competitor does not lie with other companies or derived competition, but instead the “silence” that someone can end up in. Therefore, in a combined effort of proclaimed westernism and modernization: the person comes confronted with the noise of others and shudders back into an echo chamber of truth. No inescapable reality can derive from a place without a captor, and yet it remains ever clearer to whom this can apply once again. It's always the fault of the victim, and never that of the mere 1% of the population; the minority of immediate opinion and concerns. Look around the world, and the narrowing of that field to just the scape of a mere home can become deeply unsettling to many.


When checking into the system filled by not just creators but other people in your life becomes compulsory, there is without a reasonable doubt it feels pressuring to go without the noise. To fill the gaps with the same elements as the rest comes across as the eventual outcome of what people desire and insurmountable.


Web & App services have been on a steady increase since the introduction of a developer-curated store front, deemed the App Store™ by Apple ©. When such a platform first came out, the mere bonding factor that came from just using these primitive yet fairly new platforms with continued demand itself became a new market. Holding the attention and willing nature for people to use products without restraint is the ultimate desire of anyone selling something. This, with grouped activity is what is the essential makeup of truly frictionless online consumerism and spending. With one of the world's greatest spending and most self-actualized base of population entangled inside the confines of what is but instant communication to others, buying impulse is extrapolated to greater deals. Searching for a good deal itself becomes more competitive, however it's arguable that the strengthened demand towards just purchasing increases the volume of want. On average, the greater demand of these things on mass has raised the average alongside the wish for these same companies to continue technological expansion to platform these spenders. This unchecked growth and continued demand means that there is without a doubt minimal ability for someone who is unwilling to participate to remain afloat in a marketplace of minimal effort. To each their own, and to smaller platforms, the presence of this choice comes at a greater detriment.


No right or wrong comes from merely purchasing what is cheapest, but this constant reliance on the services provided by major corporations results in an increased ability to silently increase fees and pricing without much freedom of choice.


These basic concepts of mind are easy to swallow, not a single person can truly find themselves solo or navigating a digital sphere without some form of friction.
